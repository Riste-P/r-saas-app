# Cleaning Business Management App — Feature Plan

## Core: Clients & Properties

- **Client management** — name, contact info, billing details, notes
- **Property types** — houses, buildings, individual apartments, commercial objects (restaurants, offices)
- **Building → apartment hierarchy** — a building has many apartments, each is its own billable unit with its own tenant/contact
- **Property details** — address, size (sqm), number of rooms, access instructions, key/code info

## Scheduling & Jobs

- **Recurring schedules** — weekly, biweekly, monthly per property/apartment
- **One-time jobs** — on-demand bookings
- **Calendar view** — daily/weekly/monthly overview of all jobs
- **Assign cleaners** — assign staff to specific jobs
- **Route optimization** (later) — group nearby jobs on the same day
- **Job status tracking** — scheduled, in-progress, completed, cancelled, missed

## Billing & Payments

- **Per-apartment billing** — each apartment in a building is invoiced separately
- **Payment tracking** — paid, unpaid, overdue per apartment/property
- **Payment history** — full log of who paid what and when
- **Invoice generation** — create and send invoices (PDF/email)
- **Pricing** — per property/apartment pricing, different rates for different service types
- **Overdue reminders** — flag or notify when payments are late

## Staff Management

- **Employee list** — name, contact, availability
- **Assign to jobs** — who cleans where
- **Work log** — hours worked per job/day
- **Availability/time-off** — track when staff is unavailable

## Service Types

- **Define services** — regular cleaning, deep cleaning, window cleaning, post-construction, etc.
- **Service pricing** — different rates per service type and property size
- **Checklists** — what tasks are included in each service type

## Reporting & Dashboard

- **Revenue overview** — monthly income, per client, per building
- **Unpaid/overdue summary** — quick view of outstanding payments
- **Job completion rates** — how many jobs completed vs scheduled
- **Client overview** — active clients, inactive clients, total properties

## Nice-to-Have (Later)

- **Client portal** — clients can see their schedule and payment status
- **Notifications** — SMS/email reminders before cleaning day
- **Photos** — before/after photos per job for quality tracking
- **Notes per job** — cleaner can leave notes ("broken handle in kitchen", etc.)
- **Contracts** — attach agreements per client/building
